Imposter Game

Find the imposters among you! A social deduction game where innocents know a secret word and imposters must blend in.

How to Play

Play free, unblocked, and on any device! Share a code and start playing instantly.

The Roles

😇 Innocent: Know the word. Prove it without spoiling it.
🎭 Imposter: Don't know the word. Blend in and guess it!

Win Conditions

  • • Innocents: Vote out all imposters.
  • • Imposters: Outnumber innocents OR guess the secret word.
